# philips-juicer-reverse_enginnering-
To reverse engineer and redesign a Philips Viva Collection juicer to understand product design, CAD modelling, manufacturing processes, and design for manufacture. The juicer was designed to maximise the juice output while minimising the cleaning effort. It was specifically developed 
to enable you to process a broad variety of ingredients and a wide range of recipes with confidence.

## Project Goals
- Measure and Reverse engineer the existing product
- Recreate components in CAD
- Understand manufacturing processes
- Propose engineering improvements

## General overview                                
1. Pusher with pre-clean function
2. Berry funnel (HR1837/HR1833 only)
3. Feeding tube
4. Lid
5. Filter
6. Juice collector with spout
7. Integrated pulp container
8. Safety clamps
9. Control knob
10. Cord storage compartment
11. Driving shaft
12. Motor unit
